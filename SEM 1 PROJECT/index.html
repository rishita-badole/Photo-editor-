Frequently Asked Questions

How can I upload an image for editing?

You can upload an image by clicking the "Choose Image" button and selecting the desired image from your device.

Are there specific file formats supported for uploading images?

Yes, supported formats include JPEG/JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, SVG, and PSD (Photoshop) files.

Can I apply multiple filters and effects simultaneously?

Yes, you can apply multiple filters and effects by toggling the desired options in the editor panel.
